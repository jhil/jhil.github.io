On the current state of the hackathon movement

<section data-field="body" class="e-content">
<section name="0df8" class=" section--body section--bottomCenter section-image--coverFade is-imageBackgrounded is-backgrounded is-darkBackgrounded u-imageSpectrum section--first"><div class="section-background"contenteditable="false" data-image-id="1*jvqojKu5389U-409UGKhTw.jpeg" data-width="1280" data-height="854" data-filter="red"><div class="section-backgroundImage"style="background-image: url(https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/fit/t/2000/1600/desat/multiply/red/overlay/red/gradv/29/81/55/1*jvqojKu5389U-409UGKhTw.jpeg);"></div><br></div><div class="section-coverFadePadding u-ignoreBlock"><div class="section-content" data-scroll="cover-fade"><div class="section-inner layoutSingleColumn"><h2 name="title" id="title" class="graf--h2 graf--leading">The Hackathon Boilerplate</h2><h4 name="subtitle" id="subtitle" class="graf--h4 graf-after--h2 graf--last">On the current state of the hackathon movement</h4></div></div></div><div class="section-captionContent u-ignoreBlock"></div></section><section name="7ff0" class=" section--body"><div class="section-divider layoutSingleColumn"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner layoutSingleColumn"><p name="fc78" id="fc78" class="graf--p graf--leading">No idea has swept the nation’s tech scene quicker than hackathons. It’s sexy: the notion that you can make amazing products by pushing code, pushing pixels, and pushing your teammates to complete a project in 36 hours. Movements move fast. And sometimes it’s hard enough just to keep up.</p><p name="f1d5" id="f1d5" class="graf--p graf-after--p">As the community continues to grow, university (and high school) students are scrambling to rouse their hacker populations and garner administrative support in hopes of one day planning <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">the big one</em>. A university hackathon marks their place on the hacker map as a leader, and who would want to miss out on that?</p><figure name="f374" id="f374" class="graf--figure graf-after--p"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 700px; max-height: 466px;"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder-fill" style="padding-bottom: 66.60000000000001%;"></div><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*-5lUtRKeuuwMPzFrNMZaXQ.jpeg" data-width="2048" data-height="1363" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1600/1*-5lUtRKeuuwMPzFrNMZaXQ.jpeg"></div><figcaption class="imageCaption">Epic ceremony at <a href="http://2014s.pennapps.com/" data-href="http://2014s.pennapps.com/" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" target="_blank">PennApps Spring 2014</a>. Photo: <a href="https://twitter.com/SamuelRiggs" data-href="https://twitter.com/SamuelRiggs" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" target="_blank">Sam Riggs</a></figcaption></figure><p name="5562" id="5562" class="graf--p graf-after--figure">That said, we should all be looking at how we can embrace productive differentiation.</p><blockquote name="39e4" id="39e4" class="graf--pullquote graf-after--p">Hackathons should not be confined to what others have done in the past.</blockquote><p name="9ccc" id="9ccc" class="graf--p graf-after--pullquote">Planning a hackathon <a href="http://html5boilerplate.com/" data-href="http://html5boilerplate.com/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">is like using Boilerplate</a>. Established events over the past couple years give new planners a framework to build on top of. The incredible success of <a href="http://pennapps.com/" data-href="http://pennapps.com/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">PennApps</a>, <a href="http://hackny.org/" data-href="http://hackny.org/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">hackNY</a>, and <a href="http://mhacks.org/" data-href="http://mhacks.org/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">MHacks</a> has brought out what really brings us all together. Hackers like hacking. Beyond that, the possibilities are boundless.</p><p name="821d" id="821d" class="graf--p graf-after--p graf--last">Organizers should plan with purpose. Every element of a traditional hackathon can be tweaked to match planners’ specific goals and vision. Name. Size. Location. Activities. Format. Food. Sponsors. There are so many open variables and so much space to fill.</p></div></div></section><section name="e3cc" class=" section--body"><div class="section-divider layoutSingleColumn"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner layoutSingleColumn"><p name="c4b1" id="c4b1" class="graf--p graf--leading"><a href="http://hackcon.io/" data-href="http://hackcon.io/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">At HackCon—a gathering of over 100 hackathon organizers</a> from around the nation—<a href="https://twitter.com/dlamdany" data-href="https://twitter.com/dlamdany" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Dina Lamdany</a> from <a href="http://adicu.com/" data-href="http://adicu.com/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Columbia University’s ADI</a> pushed us to think about <a href="http://www.columbia.edu/~dtl2117/hack" data-href="http://www.columbia.edu/~dtl2117/hack" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">how we manifest our values</a> through our hackathon.</p><figure name="436a" id="436a" class="graf--figure graf--layoutOutsetLeft graf-after--p"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 525px; max-height: 394px;"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder-fill" style="padding-bottom: 75%;"></div><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*F8sYvjAmeJ3P1mq3s99LSw.png" data-width="1440" data-height="1080" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/1*F8sYvjAmeJ3P1mq3s99LSw.png"></div><figcaption class="imageCaption">A slide from Dina’s presentation. “What values [underlie] a hackathon? How do those values manifest?”</figcaption></figure><p name="09d0" id="09d0" class="graf--p graf-after--figure graf--last">This inspired us to be more conscious, to take a step back and ask why.</p></div></div></section><section name="adaf" class=" section--body"><div class="section-divider layoutSingleColumn"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner layoutSingleColumn"><p name="cc84" id="cc84" class="graf--p graf--leading">We think about this every day <a href="http://bitca.mp/" data-href="http://bitca.mp/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">while planning Bitcamp</a>. Our hackathon is positioned somewhere between an experiment and a statement. We don&#39;t want to just be another hackathon clone at the University of Maryland. We want to create a new hackathon experience.</p><p name="efbe" id="efbe" class="graf--p graf-after--p">For example, to enforce our <a href="https://twitter.com/search?q=%23plustech" data-href="https://twitter.com/search?q=%23plustech" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">YOU+TECH theme</a>, we will be abandoning traditional judging system to encourage students to create projects that are inspired by their own passions and interests, instead of inspired solely by competition. Our expo event will be more similar to <a href="http://makerfaire.com/" data-href="http://makerfaire.com/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Maker Faire</a> or <a href="http://cesweb.org/" data-href="http://cesweb.org/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">CES</a>, events whose culture incites community, creativity, and innovation. We’re going all out with this concept, building up our expo to be the climactic and most exciting segment of Bitcamp.</p><figure name="2ee0" id="2ee0" class="graf--figure graf-after--p"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 700px; max-height: 466px;"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder-fill" style="padding-bottom: 66.5%;"></div><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*81Mfbl9951Yn3w-iNNiFMQ.jpeg" data-width="2048" data-height="1362" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1600/1*81Mfbl9951Yn3w-iNNiFMQ.jpeg"></div><figcaption class="imageCaption">Student experiments with <a href="http://bopit.io/" data-href="http://bopit.io/" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" target="_blank">bopit.io</a> at <a href="http://2014s.pennapps.com/" data-href="http://2014s.pennapps.com/" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" target="_blank">PennApps Spring 2014</a> expo. Photo: <a href="https://twitter.com/pvorozh" data-href="https://twitter.com/pvorozh" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" target="_blank">Polina Vorozheykina</a></figcaption></figure><p name="3f4e" id="3f4e" class="graf--p graf-after--figure graf--last">We are trying out new things that reflect our values while employing the hackathon boilerplate created by our predecessors. And we’re excited to collaborate with fellow hackers to further develop our ideas and move this community forward.</p></div></div></section><section name="82bb" class=" section--body"><div class="section-divider layoutSingleColumn"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner layoutSingleColumn"><p name="15fa" id="15fa" class="graf--p graf--leading graf--last"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">Bitcamp is a hackathon at University of Maryland on April 4-6. </em><a href="http://bitca.mp/" data-href="http://bitca.mp/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">To learn more, visit bitca.mp</em></a><em class="markup--em markup--p-em"> or hit us up at hello@bitca.mp.</em></p></div></div></section><section name="9a7e" class=" section--body section--last"><div class="section-divider layoutSingleColumn"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner layoutSingleColumn"><p name="9f8b" id="9f8b" class="graf--p graf--leading graf--last"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">I’m Jeff Hilnbrand — hit me up on </em><a href="http://twitter.com/jhilmd" data-href="http://twitter.com/jhilmd" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">Twitter</em></a><em class="markup--em markup--p-em"> and check out </em><a href="http://jhil.co" data-href="http://jhil.co" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">my portfolio</em></a><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">.
