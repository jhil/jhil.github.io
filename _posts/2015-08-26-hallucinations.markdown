<h1 class="p-name">Hallucinations</h1>
</header>
<section data-field="subtitle" class="p-summary">
For as long as I can remember, I’ve had a recurring set of dreams. I wouldn’t describe them as nightmares, they’re just peculiar.
</section>
<section data-field="body" class="e-content">

Not sure if it has a material. More of a texture, a sort of matte grain. I don’t ever touch the sphere, I just gape at its presence while standing in place. Eventually the sphere starts scaling so big, slowly and silently. I realize that it will continue growing and begin to panic. Everyone else is asleep.</p><p name="8ccf" id="8ccf" class="graf--p graf-after--p">The sphere is approaching the size of the hallway and I’m trapped. It passes the bounds of the walls and ceilings. Instead of destroying the structure, the sphere just replaces it.</p><p name="074e" id="074e" class="graf--p graf-after--p">I can’t leave, so I embrace the sphere. When it reaches my body, I enter it. During my passage, I feel the weight and touch of the sphere, but am not ever physically displaced.</p><p name="8b7e" id="8b7e" class="graf--p graf-after--p graf--last">Inside is nothing.</p></div></div></section><section name="abdd" class=" section--body"><div class="section-divider layoutSingleColumn"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--outsetColumn"><figure name="531c" id="531c" class="graf--figure graf--layoutOutsetCenter graf--leading"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 1020px; max-height: 510px;"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder-fill" style="padding-bottom: 50%;"></div><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*5fEkN0gLMW0NTx0XcsPxog.gif" data-width="1920" data-height="960" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/2000/1*5fEkN0gLMW0NTx0XcsPxog.gif"></div></figure></div><div class="section-inner layoutSingleColumn"><h3 name="7448" id="7448" data-align="center" class="graf--h3 graf-after--figure">Accelerate</h3><p name="3b44" id="3b44" class="graf--p graf-after--h3">I’m in an enclosed space, often the hallway outside my room again. I begin to walk, but the displacement is not one-to-one with my movement. With every step, I travel long distances.</p><p name="37b7" id="37b7" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Soon after, I am not controlling my movement at all. I am just going. I’m now traveling through these empty colorscapes.</p><p name="9541" id="9541" class="graf--p graf-after--p">The motion starts glitching and refreshing.
