And as a bonus, I’ll throw in cheesy football analogies, seeing as it is conference championship weekend in the NFL.</p></div></div></section><section name="57b6" class=" section--body"><div class="section-divider layoutSingleColumn"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner layoutSingleColumn"><h3 name="47c8" id="47c8" class="graf--h3 graf--leading">The Generalist</h3><p name="5daa" id="5daa" class="graf--p graf-after--h3">This person is the jack of all trades. Whether picking up new code to fill a hole or covering all the bases from the onset, a Generalist is a vastly useful team member. They always wear a smile and never complain. Generalists are typically skilled coders who love learning skills for the hell of it and helping where needed.</p><p name="1877" id="1877" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Football: <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tight_end" data-href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tight_end" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Tight End</a></p><p name="f0e3" id="f0e3" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Generalists I’ve hacked with: <a href="https://twitter.com/itsfuntobehappy" data-href="https://twitter.com/itsfuntobehappy" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Richard Higgins</a>, Kunal Sharma, Brandon Gudenius</p><figure name="b064" id="b064" class="graf--figure graf-after--p"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 700px; max-height: 472px;"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder-fill" style="padding-bottom: 67.5%;"></div><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*Zxy650lWmeSBXPSt5Ituuw.jpeg" data-width="1024" data-height="691" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1600/1*Zxy650lWmeSBXPSt5Ituuw.jpeg"></div><figcaption class="imageCaption">Maryland TE, Matt Furstenburg</figcaption></figure><h3 name="c435" id="c435" class="graf--h3 graf-after--figure">The Workhorse</h3><p name="c08d" id="c08d" class="graf--p graf-after--h3">Get the spec. Zone in. Grind. The Workhorse<a href="http://andrewdumont.me/reaching-flow" data-href="http://andrewdumont.me/reaching-flow" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank"> masters the art of flow</a>, bangs out code, and never sleeps. Never. She is usually seen with headphones on. No matter how long it takes or how many iterations are necessary, a Workhorse gets the job done.</p><p name="895b" id="895b" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Football: <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Runningback" data-href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Runningback" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Runningback</a></p><p name="83c1" id="83c1" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Workhorses I’ve hacked with: Evan Wang, <a href="https://twitter.com/jsquirrelz" data-href="https://twitter.com/jsquirrelz" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Justin Searles</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/InsideTheBlock" data-href="https://twitter.com/InsideTheBlock" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Scott Block</a></p><figure name="acf2" id="acf2" class="graf--figure graf-after--p"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 700px; max-height: 503px;"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder-fill" style="padding-bottom: 71.89999999999999%;"></div><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*xpbZ9kAdypg3YCAr009KtA.jpeg" data-width="1024" data-height="736" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1600/1*xpbZ9kAdypg3YCAr009KtA.jpeg"></div><figcaption class="imageCaption">Maryland RB, Brandon Ross</figcaption></figure><h3 name="fc46" id="fc46" class="graf--h3 graf-after--figure">The Hipster</h3><p name="bda1" id="bda1" class="graf--p graf-after--h3">A Hipster brings something unique to the table. When interesting skills are injected into an otherwise common project, teams usually get a creative edge. This is your designer on a web app, your seamstress on a hardware hack, your data junkie, your quant, and… well… anyone who knows Haskell. I relate most here, though the number of designers participating in hackathons is on the rise (!!!).</p><p name="d021" id="d021" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Football: <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Placekicker" data-href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Placekicker" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Placekicker</a></p><p name="6f3a" id="6f3a" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Hipsters I’ve hacked with: <a href="https://twitter.com/bbovenzi" data-href="https://twitter.com/bbovenzi" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Brent Bovenzi</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/duncangraham" data-href="https://twitter.com/duncangraham" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Duncan Graham</a></p><figure name="527c" id="527c" class="graf--figure graf-after--p"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 700px; max-height: 481px;"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder-fill" style="padding-bottom: 68.7%;"></div><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*va874YfPM1lWmXbU8g2cKw.jpeg" data-width="760" data-height="522" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1600/1*va874YfPM1lWmXbU8g2cKw.jpeg"></div><figcaption class="imageCaption">Maryland K, Brad Craddock</figcaption></figure><h3 name="da5f" id="da5f" class="graf--h3 graf-after--figure">The Learner</h3><p name="0eb6" id="0eb6" class="graf--p graf-after--h3">These are your newcomers, often enthusiastically participating in their first hackathon. With wide eyes, a Learner will <a href="http://www.codecademy.com/tracks/web" data-href="http://www.codecademy.com/tracks/web" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">spend much of their time on Codecademy</a> or absorbing best practices from peers. But do not count this one out, learners are often the dark horses of hackathons. They provide an invaluable, unique perspective on ideas and issues that leave other team members stumped. In other words, Learners use their naiveté to a team’s advantage (h/t <a href="https://twitter.com/mokogobo" data-href="https://twitter.com/mokogobo" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Michael Gubbels</a> for that point). And remember, you always gotta get started somewhere—check back in a few months, and they’ll be tearing up the scene!</p><p name="8d1a" id="8d1a" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Football: <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Football_League_Rookie_of_the_Year_Award" data-href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Football_League_Rookie_of_the_Year_Award" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Rookie of the Year</a></p><p name="9594" id="9594" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Learners I’ve hacked with: <a href="https://twitter.com/CHA_RULE" data-href="https://twitter.com/CHA_RULE" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Ryan Chow</a></p><figure name="9afa" id="9afa" class="graf--figure graf-after--p"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 700px; max-height: 467px;"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder-fill" style="padding-bottom: 66.7%;"></div><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*HhyIrKzDTBy2gkMJ11fKqQ.jpeg" data-width="2988" data-height="1992" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1600/1*HhyIrKzDTBy2gkMJ11fKqQ.jpeg"></div><figcaption class="imageCaption">Maryland WR, Stefon Diggs</figcaption></figure><h3 name="2011" id="2011" class="graf--h3 graf-after--figure">The Expert</h3><p name="8a42" id="8a42" class="graf--p graf-after--h3">Experts know their shit. Any question is met with a thorough answer. Experts are specialized in core codebases and are especially valuable for troubleshooting and diagnosing problems. They’re experienced, precise, and focused. An Expert is excited by the opportunity to share his tantalizing knowledge, and will often be approached by other teams who will traverse an entire hackathon floor for some help here and there.</p><p name="4509" id="4509" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Football: <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quarterback" data-href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quarterback" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Quarterback</a></p><p name="a060" id="a060" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Experts I&#39;ve hacked with: <a href="https://twitter.com/jollyswagman12" data-href="https://twitter.com/jollyswagman12" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Dan Gillespie</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/DrJorts" data-href="https://twitter.com/DrJorts" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Eric Rosenberg</a></p><figure name="86d9" id="86d9" class="graf--figure graf-after--p"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 700px; max-height: 467px;"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder-fill" style="padding-bottom: 66.7%;"></div><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*grJQW6bxUs121ZCbp1bOGg.jpeg" data-width="768" data-height="512" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1600/1*grJQW6bxUs121ZCbp1bOGg.jpeg"></div><figcaption class="imageCaption">Maryland QB, C.J. Brown</figcaption></figure><h3 name="571d" id="571d" class="graf--h3 graf-after--figure">The Visionary</h3><p name="079b" id="079b" class="graf--p graf-after--h3">A visionary inspires team members to drive towards a common goal. While they may not touch as much code (or any at all), they make significant contributions through their leadership and marketing prowess. Visionaries keep morale in check and often prepare the final presentation. We often see the entrepreneurial, collaborative type in this category, <a href="http://b.vimeocdn.com/ts/435/660/435660973_640.jpg" data-href="http://b.vimeocdn.com/ts/435/660/435660973_640.jpg" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">keeping the team effective</a>.</p><p name="e4e9" id="e4e9" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Football: <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Head_coach#American_football" data-href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Head_coach#American_football" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Head Coach</a></p><p name="df0d" id="df0d" class="graf--p graf-after--p">Visionaries I&#39;ve hacked with: <a href="http://alexmodo.com/" data-href="http://alexmodo.com/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Alex George</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/kangarushi" data-href="https://twitter.com/kangarushi" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Arushi Bhalla</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/dnosk" data-href="https://twitter.com/dnosk" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" target="_blank">Daniel Noskin</a></p><figure name="034f" id="034f" class="graf--figure graf-after--p graf--last"><div class="aspectRatioPlaceholder is-locked" style="max-width: 700px;
